SHADES OF YOU HISTORY


SHADES OF YOU was founded on February 14, 2019, by Filipino young entrepreneur Vanessa Nevaliza. On February 22, 2022, she formally announced her company. In its accompanying video, Nevaliza revealed that she's been working on the cosmetics line for the last two years, and that she "found the right partners and the right team, we now have 28 amazing people that are working for the brand. On the new venture, Nevaliza explained that Shades of You would encompass an entire lifestyle, and that it "isn't about how other people see you, it's about how you see yourself. I want us all to stop comparing ourselves to each other and to start embracing our own uniqueness. You are not defined by a photo, a like, or a comment."In an interview with Allure, Nevaliza stated that "beauty doesn't have to be defined by a like or a comment, or your body. We were always under the notion that this was going to be also about mental health and creating a safe place for people to connect. She also emphasized that her "main purpose when I started Shades of You was to break down the unrealistic standards of beauty we see in society today", motivated by the "pressure on us to be "perfect".

What makes Vanessa Nevaliza feel empowered and how she would use business to inspire women and lift up others?

Vanessa Nevaliza: "I feel most empowered when I’m creative — when I’m in a space where I can work, explore and create. To me, makeup is a way to own my creativity and a physical form of self-expression. It’s an opportunity to test different ideas, looks, and trends while serving as a personal daily ritual. Having that moment to decide who we are going to be for the day and see that come to life helps us feel like our true selves from the inside out". I created Shades of You as a means for self-expression, built on self-love and inclusion. My biggest mission of all is to inspire our community to love their own shades of beauty and imperfections by making them feel seen through every aspect of the brand - our story, products, messaging, and partnerships. Representation. Here in SHADES OF YOU We believe in the beauty of imperfections"
