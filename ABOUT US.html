ABOUT SHADES OF YOU COMPANY

Shades of You Make-up is breaking down unrealistic standards of perfection. This is makeup made to feel good in, without hiding what makes you unique because Shades of You is not about being someone else, but being who you are. Most of us don’t realise the connection between beauty and mental health, but for young entreprenuer Vanessa Nevaliza, that link is clear as day. Which is exactly why she founded beauty brand Shades of You. Her vision: To promote self-acceptance and create a space in the industry that supports mental well-being across age, gender identities, sexual orientations, race, culture, physical and mental abilities, and perspectives. Here, the young entreprenuer opens up about how growing up in the limelight has shaped her idea of beauty, her motivations on pushing ahead with the launch even at the height of a pandemic and more.
Shades of You commitment to mental health is about more than the power of feel-good beauty. The brand has set up a Shades Impact Fund as part of its commitment to give people access to the resources they need to support their mental health. Shades of You donates 1% of all sales to the Shades Impact Fund and raises additional funds with philanthropic foundations, corporate partners, and individuals to increase access to mental health services in educational settings. With makeup made to feel good in, Shades of You’s mission is to help everyone celebrate their individuality by redefining what beautiful means. It’s not about being someone else, but being who you are.
